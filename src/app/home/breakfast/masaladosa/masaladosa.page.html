Ingredients:- Dosa : 8 - 10 For Dosa Batter
  • 2 cups Idli Rice
  • 1 cup White Urad Dal
  • 7-8 grains of Methi Seeds
  • To taste Salt as per Taste
  • As needed Water
For Aloo Sabji
  • 3/4 Boiled Potatoes
  • 1 large onion finely sliced
  • 2 green chillies
  • 6/7 curry leaves
  • 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds
  • Handful coriander leaves
  • To taste Salt as per taste
  • 1/4 tsp Haldi / turmeric powder
  • Pinch Hing
  • For Peanut Chutney
  • 100-200 grams peanuts
  • 2-3 green chillies
  • 1/2 " ginger
  • To taste Salt
  • As needed Water
  • For tempering
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds
  • 7-8 curry leaves
Directions:- 01 . Wash and Soak rice and dal separately for about 7 8 hours or overnight. Add methi seeds to it. 02 . Now grind them in mixer separately. Add salt as per taste. Then put both of them in one Steel Dabba. 03 . Mix the batter well for 10 mins. 04 . Close the lid and keep it for fermenting in warm place. U can cover it with bedsheet. 05 . For Aloo Masala. Take one kadai. Put little oil. Oil when heated at mustard seeds. When it splutters add onion and green chillies. 06 . Saute till translucent. Now add boiled potatoes and mix well. 07 . To it add curry leaves, salt and Haldi. And mix it. 08 . For garnishing add coriander leaves. 09 . For Chutney.. take peanuts, green chillies, ginger salt and water as required and grinde well in mixer. 10 . Take it out in a bowl and keep one small kadai or vagara for tempering. Add oil and mustard seeds and curry leaves and pour over chutney. 11 . Now for Dosa..take the required amount of batter in pot. Add few drops of water. Don't make it watery. 12 . Keep tawa for on gas. 13 . Spread dosa batter on tawa. 14 . Add one teaspoon oil. Over it. And wait till it is golden brown. Add masala to it. 15 . Masala Dosa with Chutney is ready to be served.
