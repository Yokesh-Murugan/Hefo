Semiya Upma

Dish Name:-

Semiya Upma

  • Iron - 1.01 mg.

  • Zinc - 0.51 mg.

Ingredients:-

  • Serves : 4

  • Preparation Time : 5 Minutes

  • Cooking Time : 15 Minutes

  • 4 tsp oil

  • 2 cup vermicelli / semiya

  • water to cook

  • 1 tsp salt

  • 6 tsp oil

  • 2 tsp mustard

  • 1 tsp urad dal

  • 1 Dry Red Chilli

  • 2 tsp chana dal

  • 2 dried red chilli

  • few curry leaves

  • handful peanuts

  • 2 inch ginger (finely chopped)

  • 4 green chilli (slit)

  • 4 tbsp onion (finely chopped)

  • 1 tsp turmeric / haldi

  • 4 tbsp peas / matar

  • 4 tbsp carrot (finely chopped)

  • 1 tsp salt

  • 4 tbsp capsicum (finely chopped)

  • 4 tbsp beans (finely chopped)

  • 1 lemon

  • 4 tbsp coriander (finely chopped)

Directions:-


  1. Firstly, in a kadai heat 3 tsp oil and splutter 1 tsp mustard, ½ tsp urad dal, 1 tsp chana dal, 1 dried red chilli and few curry leaves.


  2. Also, add handful peanuts and roast till turn crunchy on low flame.


  3. Now add 1 inch ginger and 2 green chilli. saute well.


  4. Further, add 2 tbsp onion and saute slightly.


  5. Also add 2 tbsp peas, 2 tbsp carrot, 2 tbsp capsicum and 2 tbsp beans. saute for a minute.


  6. Additionally, add ½ tsp turmeric and ½ tsp salt. saute for a minute.


  7. Cover and cook for 2 minutes or till vegetables turn soft.


  8. Add in cooked semiya and mix gently. do not over mix as semiya will get mashed.


  9. Now add 2 tbsp coriander and ½ lemon. mix well.


  10. Finally, serve semiya upma with hot masala chai for morning breakfast.
